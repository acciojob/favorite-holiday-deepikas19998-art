Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is renowned for its rich history, stunning architecture, world-class art, and romantic ambiance. Known as the "City of Light", it offers a perfect blend of culture, cuisine, and scenic beauty.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – A charming hilltop neighborhood known for its artistic vibes.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A famous avenue perfect for shopping and strolling.

Must-Try Foods

  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
  • Baguette – Classic French bread that's crispy on the outside and soft inside.
  • Macarons – Delicate and colorful almond-based cookies.
  • Escargots – A unique delicacy of cooked snails with garlic butter.
  • Croissant – A buttery and flaky breakfast pastry.
