Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is known for its timeless charm, stunning architecture, and romantic atmosphere. It offers a rich cultural experience with its world-class museums, historical landmarks, and delightful cuisine.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Montmartre – A charming hilltop neighborhood with artistic flair.
  5. Champs-Élysées – A grand avenue perfect for shopping and strolling.

Must-Try Foods

  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory ingredients.
  • Baguette – Classic French bread, crispy on the outside, soft inside.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate almond-based treats.
  • Escargots – A famous delicacy of snails in garlic butter.
  • Croissant – Buttery and flaky pastry perfect for breakfast.
